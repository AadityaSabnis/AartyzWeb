Our Beginning

Born from a love
of Indian craft

Aartyz was founded by Aarti Sabnis in Nagpur with one powerful belief: India's textile heritage deserves a modern platform. What started as a small workshop fueled by passion has grown into a nationally recognized brand.

We work hand-in-hand with women artisans who have dedicated their lives to block printing. Every piece carries their story — their precision, their creativity, their legacy.

Partner With Us

What We Stand For

Our Values

🌱

Sustainability

Eco-conscious dyes, natural fabrics, and waste-minimal processes are not add-ons — they are the foundation of how block printing has always worked.

👐

Empowerment

Our artisans — predominantly women — are paid fairly, work in dignified conditions, and grow their craft alongside our brand every season.

🏺

Preservation

Block print is a language. We are committed to keeping it alive, relevant, and celebrated — passing it forward in every collection we release.

The Founder

Aarti Sabnis

Founder & Creative Director

"Every block print is a conversation between the artisan and the wearer — a thread of culture that connects us across time. Aartyz exists to make that connection louder."

Aarti has built Aartyz from the ground up, combining her deep reverence for Indian craft with a sharp eye for contemporary design. She leads every collection with the belief that the most beautiful fashion is also the most honest.
